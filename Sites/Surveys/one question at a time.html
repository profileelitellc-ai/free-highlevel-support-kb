“One Question at a Time” is a setting in the Survey Builder that shows one question per screen when someone is filling out your form. It creates a simpler, more focused experience for your audience.\


TABLE OF CONTENTS

  • What It Does
  • How to Turn It On
  • Things to Know

What It Does

When enabled:

  • Each survey question appears on a separate slide both for your visitors and in the builder preview.

  • This layout helps keep people engaged by showing only one thing at a time.

How to Turn It On

  1. Open your survey in the builder.

  2. Go to Options → Survey Settings.

  3. Toggle on “One Question at a Time.”

Once enabled, you’ll see each field on its own slide while editing just like how it appears to users.

Things to Know

  • If you’re using conditional logic, turning this on may affect how logic works. You’ll see a warning before anything changes.

  • This setting only changes how the survey is displayed it doesn’t remove or modify your questions.
